About Us

Al Batra in essence is a symbol of protection, it was the name of one of the Mail (armour) of Hazrat Muhammed (S.A.W). This name was decided to show case the ability of the disinfectant itself.
We at Al Batra believe in the safety and security of our country. The company was formed by the ideaology of the CEO Syed Irfan Hashmi, in this time of need, to provide safety to the people at an affordable price. The disinfectant is capable of containing the disease that has been spreading across the country, at an uncontrollable rate, and give you protection from the unforseen bacteria the we carry on our self.

Vision

To be the most renowned disinfectant in Pakistan.

Mission

To provide security to the people along with the good will of the customers.
